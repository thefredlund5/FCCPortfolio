About: A self-taught developer, I began my journey like most; with a love of all things technology. After years of watching others turn that passion into a life long commitment, I decided it was time for me to take the plunge. I began tinkering with HTML and CSS through a number of online sources. After discovering Codecademy, I began to feel that I could actually learn on my own. After completing the HTML, CSS, and jQuery tracks, I began creating single page web sites. These were crude but served as a foundation to future learning. Fast forward to today, I have decided to redo my learning through the Free Code Camp curriculum. This very portfolio serves as a testament to the skills that I have developed.
